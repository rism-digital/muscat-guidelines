### Subject heading (650)

The field **Subject heading** is for topics that help to describe the contents of the item.

#### Subject heading (650 $a)

Enter a subject heading, generally in English and in the plural. Enter subject headings especially if the contents of the item are not obvious from the title. This field is linked to the **Subject headings** authority file through $0.

##### Examples

- Instruments
- Madrigals
