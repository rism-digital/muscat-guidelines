### Internal note (667)

The field **Internal note** is used for information not intended for public display. It is best practice to sign your note using your initials and the date.

#### Internal note (667 $a)

Enter local notes for internal use, including for use within your own working group. Any language may be entered here. Internal notes can be used for remarks of all kinds, such as questions, comments, or research to be done. Internal notes are not published in the public RISM catalogs or available in the RISM open data. It is best practice to sign your note using your initials and the date.
